## 8. Risk Assessment

### 8.1 Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Badge rendering issues** | Medium | Low | Test on multiple browsers, provide fallback text |
| **Mobile layout breaks** | Medium | High | Extensive mobile testing, responsive tables |
| **Load time increase** | Low | Medium | Optimize images, lazy load badges |
| **Link rot** | Low | Medium | Regular link checking, use relative paths |
